What is Tomorrow Toonies?

First of all, Welcome to our Official Website. Tomorrow Toonies are a NFT & Metaverse Project that want to bring the general population into the NFT space via simple and fun activities. As we are a very small team we plan on working small things such as this website (Currently using Web1 Technology) and our Discord Server where we announce all the latest project goals and ideas.
