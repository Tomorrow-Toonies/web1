What is Tomorrow Toonies?

Tomorrow Toonies are a NFT & Metaverse Project with the goal of securing the better future for all using old, modern and future technology together to make a flowing workspace for all. Through our Discord Server we provid the quickest and easiest way to contact us about any issues or throughts about our goals & services and love to be told what needs to be improved, So maybe join us there.

When did Tomorrow Toonies start?

We started work on our first NFTs around 2022-01-15 (GMT) releasing the First NFT early morning of 2022-01-16 (GMT). Our full vision took about a week to see the possibility of what we could do using the Polygon Blockchain and our ideas just keep coming for what this technology could do for people. Along with what other services we could bring to people in the near and far future needless to say we have a lot in the works and we hope you enjoy it and join us in this journey.
