What is Tomorrow Toonies?

Tomorrow Toonies are a small team working on a NFT & Metaverse Project that want to get the general population involved in the NFT space to show the everyday use of these amazing assets. We're working on growing with our Discord Server being one of our key goals to grow and expand as a community is nothing without the people. Also we plan on working on Metaverse Events via games such as Echo VR, Rec Room & Fortnite in the future for players to compete for a custom made (for the event) NFT with our whole project being Free-To-Join and no costs for anyone directly from us.


When did Tomorrow Toonies Start?

We started work on the first NFTs around 2022-01-15 releasing the First NFT early morning of 2022-01-16 (GMT). Our full vision took about a week to see the possibility of what we could do using the Polygon Blockchain and our ideas just keep coming for what this technology could do for people.
